Learn More XM Fitness Rig Systems

The XM-A6 is a 24 x 6 base configuration that gives you 6 complete lifting stations and 10 pull up stations. Additionally the inside of the rig features a monkey bar system that is 9ft high and 24ft in length.

It doesn't stop there, the Xtreme Monkey Rig system accommodates over 15 different attachments allowing you to customize your rig system to the ultimate training machine.

All XM Rig configurations feature components with 11 gauge steel and a powder coated finish that is built to last. Our 9ft Uprights are 3x3 and feature lasercut 1in holes with 2in spacing and come with concrete floor anchors. Xtreme Monkey Rig Systems are Commercial rated but priced affordably for your garage or home gym.

Full set of hardware is always included along with concrete floor bolts to secure the uprights to the ground. A 5/8" drill bit is required and is not included with purchase.
